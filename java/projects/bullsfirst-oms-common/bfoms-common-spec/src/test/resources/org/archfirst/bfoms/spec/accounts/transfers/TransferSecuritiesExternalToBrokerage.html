Transfer Securities - External to Brokerage

Example

When 100 shares of APPL are transfered from an external account to a newly opened brokerage account, the following outcomes take place:

  • a new lot of APPL with quantity 100 is created in the brokerage account
  • a securities transfer of APPL with quantity -100 is recorded in the external account
  • a securities transfer of APPL with quantity 100 is recorded in the brokerage account
