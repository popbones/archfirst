Matching AllOrNone Orders

An AllOrNone order cannot be matched with another order whose quantity is less than its own.

Example 1 (AllOrNone Buy Order)

When the orders shown below are placed in the AAPL book

Creation Time Client OrderId Side Instrument Quantity Type Limit Price GTC AllOrNone
07/31/2009 09:00:00 SEAM-1000 Buy AAPL 1000 Market Y
07/31/2009 09:00:02 SEAM-1010 Sell AAPL 100 Market

the orders will not match because of the AllOrNone constraint on the buy order. The final position of the book will looks like this:

Buy Stack

Creation Time Client OrderId Side Instrument Quantity Type LimitPrice Status
07/31/2009 09:00:00 SEAM-1000 Buy AAPL 1000 Market New

Sell Stack

Creation Time Client OrderId Side Instrument Quantity Type LimitPrice Status
07/31/2009 09:00:02 SEAM-1010 Sell AAPL 100 Market New

0 fills will occur.

Example 4 (Matching Attempts Continue After A Mismatch)

Given that current market price of AAPL is 160.00, when the orders shown below are placed in the AAPL book

Creation Time Client OrderId Side Instrument Quantity Type Limit Price GTC AllOrNone
07/31/2009 09:00:00 SEAM-1000 Buy AAPL 1000 Market
07/31/2009 09:00:02 SEAM-1010 Buy AAPL 3000 Market
07/31/2009 09:00:04 SPRG-1020 Sell AAPL 2000 Market Y

the final position of the book will looks like this:

Buy Stack

Creation Time Client OrderId Side Instrument Quantity Type LimitPrice Status
07/31/2009 09:00:00 SEAM-1000 Buy AAPL 1000 Market New
07/31/2009 09:00:02 SEAM-1010 Buy AAPL 3000 Market PartiallyFilled

Sell Stack

Creation Time Client OrderId Side Instrument Quantity Type LimitPrice Status
